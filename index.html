At Pro Ridge Roofing, we know the decision to install a new roof is difficult, and we’re here to make the process as convenient as possible. One of the challenges property owners face is getting their new roof paid for by insurance. Did the most recent storm damage the roof or was the damage pre-existing? The insurance company will send their adjuster out to check. Pro Ridge Roofing has spent years developing good working relationships with insurance companies. We can combine that relationship with our knowledge of the little details that are so important in insurance matters. We take the load of dealing with insurance carriers off your shoulders and put it on ours.
